FEATURES

This product is a website that builds a community of DIY’ers to support and celebrate their projects, while providing a unique targeted market for speciality suppliers and contractors.

TARGET

Targeted to the Gen Yer's, this site is designed for first-time home buyers who are interested in trying their hand in home improvement, and want to share their great designs, updated homes, and impressive handiwork with their friends and family.

BENEFITS

For DIY’ers

  • Log and track the projects that you do
  • Share your progress and results with others
  • Catalogue your materials and where you got them
  • Get advice & ideas, receive tips from other users
  • Find key resources to make your project a success

For suppliers & service providers

  • Find those who are looking for your products and services
  • Establish yourself as a key resource or a knowledge leader
  • Targeted marketing to passionate DIY’ers across the country
